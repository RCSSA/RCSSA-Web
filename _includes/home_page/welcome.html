欢迎来到 RCSSA 的主页。莱斯大学中国学生学者联谊会 (RCSSA) 是在莱斯大学正式注册的非政治、非宗教、非营利、服务性综合性学生社团组织。自成立以来，RCSSA 为 Rice 大学中国学生学者提供了生活、学习和职业规划上的帮助，组织了丰富多彩的娱乐活动。对外 RCSSA 则积极参与各项校内校外交流活动，弘扬中国文化。Rice 的中国学生学者现有八百余人，占学校总人口的10%以上，是学校最大的国际学生学者团体。

Welcome to the homepage of Rice Chinese Students and Scholars Association (RCSSA). RCSSA is an official student organization registered at Rice University with no political, religious or profit-driven purpose. Since its foundation, RCSSA has provided Chinese students' with academic, social, and professional support, in addition to organizing various events on a yearly basis. Externally, RCSSA is devoted in establishing inter-college level exchanges as well as cultural exchanges. There are around 800 Chinese students and scholars at Rice University currently, accounting for more than 10% of the school’s total population. RCSSA thus serves the biggest ethnic community on campus.
